Coyote

Though smaller than it's close relative the Grey Wolf, Coyotes have a larger Braincase.

  • Scientific Name: Canis latrans
  • Average Weight: 15-44 lb
  • Number of Subspecies: 19
  • Habitat: Mostly Grasslands

With a varied diet and flexible social organization (can live in family units or loosely knit packs of unrelated coyotes), the Coyote is an incredibly well-adapted animal whose population-expansion continues even today.
